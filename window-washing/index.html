WINDOW WASHING

for homes and businesses in the south bay area.

We offer professional window cleaning services for your home, apartment or business.

As one of the premier window washing services in the South Bay Area, we have the necessary experience and resources to help our customers no matter their requirements. With years of experience using eco-friendly products and professional grade window cleaning tools, we ensure the beauty of your property with windows that sparkle and last.

How we clean your windows

We use deionized water to help with the longevity of your windows while ensuring they remain free of water stains. The water is fed through an extended carbon fiber pole with a gentle brush cleaner. This method ensures the windows are stain-free, and the brush allows us to better clean debris off windows without extra wear. For cleaning, we use chemical-free, environmentally friendly soaps of the highest grade for quality results. With the water pole's length, we can reach high or difficult-to-access places that would otherwise require a ladder. This helps us lower risk and complete the job more efficiently. Inside your space, we use strip washers and squeegees, taking care to ensure the windows are stain-free. We also make sure to wash the dust off the screens.

What we do for our customers

Rogers Windows and Gutters can serve a variety of customer needs, with spaces such as homes, car dealerships, apartments, businesses, and offices.
We work on various types of windows including storefront windows, skylights, and storm windows. If you have a need not mentioned, we pride ourselves on our flexibility and adaptability, and will find a solution that works best for you.

The Benefits of Window Washing

Improved light, mood and atmosphere

Its essential to have clean windows for the energy inside your home. Clean windows can brighten the views, improve color, and increase the mood of the space. 

Improve the exterior look of your home or building

Clean windows on the exterior of buildings and homes improve the perception of ones home or business. This can help with the potential value of a space.

Improves the look of the business

Businesses with clean windows signal quality and care to potential customers. Workers need good views and lighting. Increase the customer experience by looking bright and shiny!
