WINDOW CLEANING

The easiest way to get beautiful lighting with a crystal-clear view? A good window cleaning from Rogers Windows and Gutters!
We use state-of-the-art window cleaning equipment, including a gentle brush cleaner with a water-fed pole and deionized water, along with patient squeegee techniques – we'll make sure your home or business gets the view it deserves.

GUTTER & ROOF CLEANING

Dirt and debris accumulating on your roof over time can cause composite tile rot or roof failure. Dirty gutters can exacerbate roof decay and can themselves corrode, causing damage around the house and requiring replacement. We provide comprehensive cleaning of your gutters and roof, including moss removal. We take thorough safety precautions and treat your roof as if it were our own.

PRESSURE WASHING

All surfaces around your house will be cleaned to perfection. We do our best to remove any dirt, decay, mold, and moss to ensure the longevity of the building and beautiful-looking surfaces. Increase the look, the longevity and, importantly, the safety of your surfaces. Just name what you need washed, and we'll do it!

SOLAR PANEL CLEANING

Cleaning your solar panels is of vital importance to their longevity. To maximize energy absorption, it's important to keep solar panels regularly cleaned.
We clean solar panels on homes, properties, and buildings, and can accommodate just about any kind of configuration.
